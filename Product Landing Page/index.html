About

Bang Ye-dam is a South Korean singer and music producer who was born on May 7, 2002 in Seoul. She comes from a family of musicians and started in the entertainment industry at an early age. Her popularity increased after becoming the runner-up in the K-pop Star 2 competition in 2012. Ye-dam then joined YG Entertainment as a trainee and eventually debuted as a member of the group Treasure in 2020. Known by the nickname "God Yedam" for her outstanding vocal ability, she also contributed as a producer to several songs. In November 2022, Ye-dam decided to leave Treasure and focus on her career as a music producer.

Music

Bang Yedam (방예담) - Fallin'

Release Song : August 29, 2024 salviaaaa

Bang Yedam created the song "Fallin'" as part of his second mini album, "GOOD ViBES", which was released on August 29, 2024. The song depicts the feeling of falling deeply in love and longing for someone who has left. Through emotional lyrics, Yedam expresses regret for lost opportunities and a desire to return to the past. He describes himself as still falling in love even though the relationship has ended, showing the theme of unrequited love and the difficulty of forgetting someone who mattered. The song reflects a personal experience or feeling that Yedam wants to convey to his listeners, inviting them to feel the same emotions through the melody and profound lyrics. )
