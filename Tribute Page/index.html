Mark Zuckerberg: The Innovator Who Changed the World

Mark Zuckerberg, co-founder and CEO of Facebook.

Mark Zuckerberg is an American computer programmer and internet entrepreneur who co-founded Facebook, Inc. in 2004. He is known for revolutionizing the social networking world and becoming one of the youngest billionaires.

Through Facebook, Zuckerberg has made significant contributions to the way people connect and communicate globally, becoming a driving force in the development of the modern internet era.
